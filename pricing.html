Hubaloo's FAQs

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) as well as PayPall.

How long does the Free Trial last?

The free trial for our Premium Account lasts a full 30 days.

How will Hubaloo save me time?

By streamlining your workflow with a single log-in, and centralized hub for updating multiple social media platforms with one easy click.

What specialists do you have available for support?

We have a range of copy editors, graphic designers, marketing-gurus and social-media coaches on our customer support and consultant teams.

How do I integrate Hubaloo with my existing accounts?

Get started on our setup page–you will need to provide your login information for the social media accounts you wish to connect.

Can Hubaloo work for a team of people?

Absolutely! Just create a centralized login for your accounts and you can add team-members to timelines for routine social-media updates.
